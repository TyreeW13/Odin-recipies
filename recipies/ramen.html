RAMEN NOODLES

A lovely picture of some ramen noodles from my computer!

Ramen noodles is my favourite go to out and about dish, I simply love it and will declare war against anyone that says it isnt nice p.s. im only joking XD.

ingredients

  • pork
  • Naruto aka Fish cake
  • herbs and spices
  • Good old broth

Steps to follow:

  1. First start by creating your broth
  2. heat up your noodles and let them simmer
  3. cook the pork any style you like
  4. when plating add the noodle and veggies, pork then the hot broth and garnish
