JERK CHICKEN

A lovely picture of some chicken from the internet!

Jerk chicken is primarily done in a big jerk pan and is local cusine to the Jamaican people, its spicy and nice with some hot sauce.

ingredients

  • Chicken
  • scotch bonnet pepper
  • herbs and spices
  • oil

Steps to follow:

  1. First start by cleaning your chicken and placing it in a bowl
  2. blend up your scotch bonnet peppers, herbs and spices and pour over your chicken
  3. Let that marinate for a few days before you cook in a smoker grill
  4. after that cook the chicken and enjoy, make sure to wash the chicken with sauce as you go
