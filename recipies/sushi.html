Juicy Sushi

A lovely picture of some sushi thats well presented!

Again sushi is easily on my top 5 foods to eat, its fresh, juicy full of flavour with soy sauce not to mention with wasabi and picked ginger, Give it a try!!!

ingredients

  • Rice
  • Sea weed rapping
  • filling of your choice
  • some sake to wash it down

Steps to follow:

  1. lay your seaweed out
  2. then goes your rice
  3. add your toppings
  4. and roll like youve never rolled before
  5. if you fail, dont fear as takeaway is here!
